About This Course

This course provides a rigorous introduction to real analysis, covering the foundational concepts of completeness, sequences, series, and topology of the real numbers. Through careful proofs and problem-solving, you will develop a deep understanding of how the real number system works and the tools we use to study functions and their properties.

Each handout is designed for active learning. Work through the definitions, theorems, and examples, and attempt the exercises before checking the solutions.
