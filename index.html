About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Millsaps College. My research interests are in functional analysis and operator theory, with a focus on n-potent operators, projections on Banach spaces, and spectral decompositions.

I am particularly interested in undergraduate research, proof-based instruction, and building strong mathematical intuition alongside rigor.

Research

My work explores structural and spectral properties of operators on Banach spaces, including eigenprojections, Riesz projections, and decompositions associated with n-potent operators.
