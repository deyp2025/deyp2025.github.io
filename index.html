About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Millsaps College. I work in functional analysis and operator theory, studying projections and related spectral structure on Banach spaces.

I am especially enthusiastic about proof-based teaching and mentoring undergraduates toward research-level thinking, with an emphasis on building intuition and rigor side by side.

Research

My research lies in functional analysis and operator theory, focusing on operators on Banach spaces. I am particularly interested in projections and isometries, and in understanding their structural and spectral properties. My current work explores how geometric features of Banach spaces influence the existence and behavior of spectral and Riesz projections, as well as related operator classes. I also study problems that arise at the interface of operator theory and other areas of analysis.
